Digvijay Patankar

  • Education

I am Digvijay Patankar, currently a research scholar in the Department of Earthquake Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee, India. I am trying to figure out an effective and easy design strategy for the friction dampers to be used in buildings. During an event of earthquake, these dampers are expected to dissipate some of the energy supplied by earthquake to the structure. This will reduce the damage to structure in case of a severe earthquake. Other than this I have interest in some other domains which includes computer programming, $\LaTeX$, Linux and Astronomy.
